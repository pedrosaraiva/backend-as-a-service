As part of the launch of the Developer Toolbox, we’ve released a new product called Typicals.  If you’re working with customers to calculate energy savings and you “typically” ask for their usage data, Genability Typicals will be of some use to you.What is Typicals Data?Glad you asked.  Typicals is a database we’ve compiled of “typical” costs, usage and rates for a specific building type in a specific geography.
